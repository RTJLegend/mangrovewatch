About the Project

ABOUT
MANGROVEWATCH

MangroveWatch is a student-led awareness initiative dedicated to exposing the accelerating destruction of mangrove forests across urban coastlines. From the Gulf to South Asia, these irreplaceable ecosystems are being cleared for roads, ports, and real estate — silently, rapidly, and with devastating consequences.

Through our weekly magazine, interactive data, field events, and community outreach, we aim to make the mangrove crisis impossible to ignore. Every mangrove lost is a coastline more vulnerable, a fishery collapsed, a community left unprotected.

Key Facts
🌊

Natural Coastal Shield

Mangrove root systems reduce wave energy by up to 70%, protecting coastlines from storm surges, tsunamis, and erosion. Removing them directly exposes millions of people to flooding risk.

🐠

Marine Nursery

Over 80% of commercial fish species depend on mangrove habitats at some life-cycle stage. Their destruction collapses fisheries that coastal communities rely on for food and income.

The Urban Threat

Roads, bridges, ports, and real-estate developments routinely encroach on mangrove zones. "Land reclamation" projects permanently erase ecosystems that took centuries to form.

A Humanitarian Issue

Coastal flooding worsens. Fish populations collapse. Carbon stored for millennia is released. Communities dependent on fishing and coastal protection lose their livelihoods and safety.

Mission Briefing
Mangrove Conservation Scavenger Hunt
Qurum Beach, Muscat, Oman · After Summer Holidays 2026

A team-based field operation at one of Muscat's most important mangrove habitats. Participants work in squads to complete conservation challenges — collecting waste, recording field data, identifying species, and navigating ecological checkpoints — all while contributing to the protection of coastal ecosystems across Oman. Entry: Free for All.

🧹
Coastal waste collection & removal at marked zones
📊
Field data: species counts, water clarity, root density
🗺️
Navigation checkpoints across the Qurum mangrove belt
📸

🌱
